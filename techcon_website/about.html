History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small conference dedicated to emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into a global event that attracts thousands of attendees from all over the world. Our journey reflects the rapid evolution of technology, from early software innovations to the rise of AI, IoT, and blockchain technologies.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to create a collaborative environment where technology leaders, developers, and enthusiasts come together to exchange ideas and shape the future. We are dedicated to fostering innovation, diversity, and sustainability in the tech industry.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, visionary entrepreneur and innovator, has been a keynote speaker at TechCon several times, sharing insights on space exploration, electric vehicles, and artificial intelligence.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Meta (formerly Facebook), has spoken at TechCon on the importance of leadership and innovation in the digital age.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, the CEO of Microsoft, shared his vision on the future of cloud computing and AI, inspiring thousands of attendees with his forward-thinking approach.
